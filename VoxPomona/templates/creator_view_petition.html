Increase Support for Computer Science Department

Started by Jerry Yan (PO '18)

Introduction

Student interest in Computer Science has surged in recent years. At its current size, the department cannot meet student interest and often has to turn away non-majors who may wish to take classes in the Department. We feel that the Department needs increased financial support from the College in order to meet rising student interest in the field.


We ask that the administration do the following:

Clause 1

Increase the budget of the CS department by 10% for the 2017-2018 school year and by 5% each of the following 4 years.
